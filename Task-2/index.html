About Us

Since the time immemorial, the world has been mesmerized by this beautiful beverage called Chai. It started somewhere around the North-Eastern areas of India and now it is the most consumed drink in the world, after water. India is the country of tea lovers. And they all love Chai from the bottom of their hearts. Tea Post was envisioned with the dream of reaching out to such tea lovers and offering them a great experience of having Chai. According to a study, Indians consume about 25% of the tea produced in the world, every year. And that's because it makes a big & powerful impact on one's mind, which is amazingly refreshing. This is evident from the unprecedented rise in the number of Tea Post outlets which grew to 250+ within the first decade. Today, Tea Post is one of the largest Tea Café chains in India — serving more than 15 varieties of Chai and a wide range
